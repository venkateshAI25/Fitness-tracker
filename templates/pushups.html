How to perform proper push-ups:

  • Start in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width
  • Keep your body in a straight line from head to heels
  • Lower your body until your chest nearly touches the floor
  • Keep your elbows at about a 45-degree angle to your body
  • Push back up to the starting position with full control
  • Keep your core engaged throughout the movement
  • Don't let your hips sag or rise too high
  • Breathe out as you push up, breathe in as you lower down
