How to perform proper squats:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, toes pointing slightly outward
  • Keep your chest up and back straight
  • Push your hips back as if sitting down in a chair
  • Lower your body until your thighs are parallel to the floor
  • Keep your knees in line with your toes (don't let them collapse inward)
  • Keep your weight in your heels, not your toes
  • Push through your heels to return to standing position
